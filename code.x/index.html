Frequently Asked Questions
How does the AR visualization work? ▼
The AR visualization overlays planetary shapes and data in real-time for interactive learning.
How accurate is the AI model? ▼
The CNN model is trained on NASA datasets and uses explainable AI to ensure reliable predictions.
Can beginners also detect planets? ▼
Beginners can learn interactively, while the AI model assists in professional-level detection.
How does the AI detect exoplanets for researchers? ▼
The CNN model analyzes photometric light curves, identifying stellar flux variations, transit signals, and size features. It also estimates radius, density, and orbital parameters to compare with NASA datasets for identifying new planets.
How is trust in AI ensured? ▼
We use explainable AI techniques including uncertainty estimation, confidence scoring, and LIME, highlighting critical data regions influencing predictions.
